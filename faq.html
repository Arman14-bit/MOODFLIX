QUESTION

What is MoodFlix?

MoodFlix is a movie recommendation platform that suggests movies based on your current mood like happy, sad, romantic, scared, or bored.

How do I get a movie suggestion?

Select your mood from the homepage, and MoodFlix will instantly show you a movie that matches how you're feeling with poster, description, and trailer.

Will I get different movies if I click again?

Yes! Each time you click your mood, you may get a different movie recommendation so you can explore more options.

Can I watch the full movie here?

No, MoodFlix only provides trailers and descriptions. It does not host or stream full movies. However, it helps you discover what to watch next.

Do I need to create an account to use MoodFlix?

No account is needed. MoodFlix works instantly just pick your mood and get movie suggestions right away.

Where do the trailers come from?

MoodFlix uses official trailers hosted on YouTube. Clicking the "Watch Trailer" button opens the video in a new tab.

How does MoodFlix decide which movie to show?

Movies are selected randomly from a mood-based list curated manually. Each mood has a set of films tailored to match its vibe.
